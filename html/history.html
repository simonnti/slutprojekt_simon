History of The Karla Tower

The Karla Tower has a rich history that dates back to its initial conception. Originally envisioned as a modern landmark, the project evolved through various stages of planning, revision, and legal reviews before finally gaining approval and beginning construction. Over the years, the tower has come to represent not just architectural ambition but also resilience through its journey.

Construction Timeline

September 2017 - Detailed plan accepted by the Gothenburg county board.
November 2017 - Plans appealed against by the Swedish land and environmental court.
December 2017 - Plans regained legal power.
2018 - Design refinements and contractor bidding phase.
